manabu shimada is a Psychoacoustic Strategist, Composer, Sound Designer, and co-founder of Land ahoy studio, working between London and Tokyo. His practice explores how sound shapes memory, emotion, and brand perception. He has crafted sonic identities for Sony, Bao Bao Issey Miyake, and Swarovski, led the sonic branding for GAC Motor and designed all 48 brand sounds, and exhibited sound art with Es Devlin.
